The Story Behind the Vision

Film director, international DJ and curator, Samuell Goldfinch crafts cinematic worlds and live experiences that move people.

About Samuell

Creative director, filmmaker, and international DJ based in Paris.

Founder of Blaze Production and Kolasi, Samuell brings stories to life through music, visuals, and innovation. His agencies have worked with Brunch Festival, MIPIM Cannes, Transdev, France Tourisme, and over 50 international DJs and 20 venues worldwide.

Known for performances at Le Speakeasy Paris & Cannes, Gate Club, Silencio, Saint-Tropez, San Sebastián, and Hard Rock Hotel Punta Cana, Samuell continues to unite art, technology, and music to create experiences that move people.

Milestones

About Samuell Goldfinch

Samuell Goldfinch is a creative director, filmmaker and international DJ. Starting in camera and videography as a teenager, he progressed to directing short films, music videos and event productions. He founded Kolasi and Blaze Production to combine film, music and live experiences into cohesive creative projects.

About Kolasi

Kolasi is a creative booking and talent agency that shapes brands’ visual and audio identity through artistic direction, DJ bookings, live shows and PR strategy. With a network of 50+ international DJs and partnerships across Europe, the Middle East and South America, Kolasi curates unforgettable events and elevated live experiences.

About Blaze Production

Blaze Production creates cinematic films and photography for weddings, editorial and commercial projects. Trusted by MIPIM Cannes, Brunch Festival, Transdev and France Tourisme, Blaze focuses on precision, emotion and storytelling to turn moments into lasting visual work.

Artist Statement

Kolasi is the live identity through which Samuell translates cinematic sensibilities into sound. Through melodic house sets, curated line-ups and carefully designed programming, he shapes the sonic atmosphere for clubs, festivals and private celebrations. Kolasi values flow, emotional arc and the fit between music, light and space. A natural extension of the film work produced under Blaze Production.
